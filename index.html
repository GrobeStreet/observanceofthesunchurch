Here is what you do

It is almost suspiciously simple.

No app to download. No mantra to learn. No teacher to find. Just you, the sky, and quiet.

1

Show up 20 minutes early

Check the sunrise or sunset time. Go somewhere you can see the light change: a beach, rooftop, hill, porch, or window.

2

Phone away. Not silent — away.

This is where restraint begins: with your own attention.

3

Watch the Sun cross the horizon

At sunrise the world shifts into day. At sunset the last sliver disappears. You are choosing to notice.

4

Stay 20 minutes after

Do not leave right away. Let the quiet after the moment do its work.

5

Come back tomorrow

Repetition, not intensity. The practice deepens because you returned.

A movement against noise

People are choosing stillness.

This is a deliberate refusal of optimization, automation, and algorithmic living. No sermons. No songs. No leaders. Just presence. Just return.

In the darkness, we hold the Light within to find our way.
